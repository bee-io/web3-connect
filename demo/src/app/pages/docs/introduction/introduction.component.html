Web3-Connect

Easy way to connect users to Decentralized Applications.

Web3-Connect provides a fast and simple solution for integrating multiple wallets and supporting various blockchain networks in your project. By incorporating pre-built components for over 35 different hardware and software wallets, Web3-Connect streamlines your development process and eliminates unnecessary difficulties.

Features#

  • Connection of Multiple Wallets and Accounts Enable your app users to link several wallets and multiple accounts inside each wallet simultaneously.
  • Support for Multiple Chains Enable users to easily switch between different networks and chains.
  • Wallet Management Center A persistent interface for managing wallet connections and networks, with a responsive version designed for mobile devices.
  • Real-time Notifications Receive immediate transaction notifications for all transaction states on the connected wallet addresses.
  • Standardization of Wallet Providers All wallet modules provide a provider that conforms to the specifications of EIP-1193 , EIP-1102 , EIP-3085 and EIP-3326 specifications.
  • Dynamic Loading To support multiple wallets in your app, you need many dependencies. Web3Connect loads a wallet and its dependencies dynamically only when the user selects it, resulting in minimal bandwidth usage.

Supported Networks#

Web3-Connect is compatible with all networks built on the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM). Adding support for a new network is straightforward by including its specific information in the designated "Chains" section during the initialization process. Additional details on initialization options can be found for further guidance.

Wallet Modules#

Enhance the capabilities and support of your web3-connect integration by incorporating additional wallet modules like Wallet Connect or Ledger. These modules expand the range of options and functionalities available to you. You can find a comprehensive list of all available modules on the web3-connect documentation website, accessible through the subpages on the left-hand side.

We suggest incorporating the Injected Wallets module to establish connections with popular wallets such as Metamask, Trust, Coinbase Wallet, and others immediately. This module enables seamless integration with these wallets, allowing users to easily access their accounts and interact with your application.
